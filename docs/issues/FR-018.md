# FR-018: Reward Credits and Trust Levels

Status: Future  
Labels: incentives, future

## Requirement

Reward credits or trust levels incentivize high-quality reports without revenue sharing complexity.

## User Story

As a participating school, I want recognition for useful reporting, so that contribution feels valuable.

## Proposed Solution

Later reward good reports and penalize abuse without revenue sharing.

## Implementation Flow

Trust scoring, reward credits, report quality rules.

## Technical Approach

Follow the stack and data model in `docs/Project_Requirements.md` and the rules in `docs/Coding_Guidelines.md`. Use server-side validation, school-scoped permissions, audit logging where sensitive, and transactional database operations for wallet/payment work.

## Key Considerations

Keep the MVP focused on clearance requests, not full school management. Use safe product language, minimize child/parent data, and avoid presenting missing records as clearance.
